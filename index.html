Connected Textures

Pick all

These packs use a different method of connecting textures than in mods you might be used to such as OptiFine. They use a cullfacing method which means the connecting textures will connect to all blocks, not just themselves. This isn't a bug; it's an unintended and unresolvable side effect of this vanilla method.
